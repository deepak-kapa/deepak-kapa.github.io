  1. COMPAct: Computational Optimization and Automated Modular Design of Planetary Actuators
    Deepak Kapa, Aman Singh, Suryank Joshi, and 1 more author
    Under Review - IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA), 2026

  2. A Novel Energy Efficient Quadruped with Direct-Drive Five-Bar Leg Architecture
    Deepak Kapa, Aman Singh, Shashank Ramesh, and 2 more authors
    Under Review - IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA), 2026
    Under Review
    Abs

    We designed and simulated an asymmetric 5-bar quadruped mechanism aimed at improving power efficiency during locomotion. Developed a detailed CAD model of an 8-DOF quadruped and performed dynamic gait simulations in MuJoCo under position control to evaluate motor power consumption and total cost of transport against traditional 2R configurations.

2025

  1. Pancake 3K Planetary Actuator: A Lightweight, Compact, and Low-Backlash Actuator
    Deepak Kapa, Aman Singh, and Shishir N.Y. Kolathaya
    In IEEE-RAS 24th International Conference on Humanoid Robots (LBR), 2025
    Abs

    We propose a novel configuration for Wolfrom and compound planetary actuators that reduces the mass and axial width of the second stage by nearly 50% while maintaining the same load capacity and efficiency. The design has been validated through steady-state torque analysis and kinematic modeling, and is currently being fabricated for experimental characterization.

  2. Comparison between External and Internal Single Stage Planetary Gearbox Actuators for Legged Robots
    Aman Singh, Deepak Kapa, Prasham Chedda, and 1 more author
    In Advances in Robotics 2025 (AIR), 2025
    Abs

    A comprehensive comparison study of external and internal planetary gearbox configurations for legged robot applications, analyzing efficiency, torque density, and mechanical characteristics.

  3. A Co-Design Framework for Energy-Aware Monoped Jumping with Detailed Actuator Modeling
    Aman Singh, Aasta Mishra, Deepak Kapa, and 2 more authors
    In IEEE-RAS 24th International Conference on Humanoid Robots, 2025
    Abs

    We developed a detailed actuator and link mass model integrated with MuJoCo to build a three-stage co-design optimization framework for a monoped robot using CMA-ES genetic algorithms. Achieved a 50% reduction in mechanical energy for a 0.8 m jump, demonstrating the value of simulation-driven mechanical optimization in improving locomotion efficiency.
